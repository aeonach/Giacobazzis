We like to call everything which is made here in Giacobazzi's the "Rosticceria". That is the Italian word for some old and rather popular Italian shops which prepared wonderful specialities for greedy foodies and tasty meals for regular customers . We like to think our Rosticceria still keeps that flag high.

For more info on our range of dishes, please email us for a copy of our Buffet List on info@giacobazzis.co.uk
