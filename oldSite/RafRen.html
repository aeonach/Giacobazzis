Raff and Renata opened Giacobazzi's Delicatessen in February 1991. Things were not easy in the beginning: Hampstead already had several delis, the recession was in full flow and two days after opening it started snowing!

Raff comes from the Apennine mountains of Northern Italy, in between Modena and Bologna. Renata is London born but is very proud of her Italian blood!

Her mother was from the same village as Raff and her father is from Parma.
