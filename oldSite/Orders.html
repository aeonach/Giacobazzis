ORDERS AND DELIVERIES

 

PLACING AN ORDER:

Email us at: info@giacobazzis.co.uk

Telephone us: 020 7267 7222

(if you get the answerphone please leave your name and phone number and

we will call you back as soon as possible - messages can be left at any time day or night).

Please leave your name, address and phone number with your order list.

Giacobazzi's does not sell, trade or rent your personal information in any form

to any outside parties.

We can prepare certain orders within a few hours but 3 days' notice is required for our homemade dishes.

Please refer to the order details given for each product.

We aim to hold all items in stock at all times. However if products become

unexpectedly popular they may be out of stock for a short while.

We will advise you of this when you place your order.


We do not take credit card payments over the phone, please contact us and

we will give you our bank details in order to make a bank transfer.

COURIER DELIVERY
Our couriers deliver throughout the UK.

Notice and restrictions: there are some restrictions on which items can be sent by courier.

Please refer to the order details given for each product.

Delivery times and details:
Delivery cost for packages upto 10kg weight = £10.00

Each kilogram over 10kg = £2.00

We aim to get your order to you within 2-3 working days of receiving it.

Orders over £75.00 (to the same address) will be delivered free of charge; the weight must be under 10 Kg.
